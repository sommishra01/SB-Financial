What is SIP Planning?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is one of the most effective ways to invest in mutual funds. It allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly and build wealth over time through compounding.

Why SIP is Important for Investors

  • Disciplined investing habit
  • Reduces market timing risk
  • Affordable for beginners
  • Power of compounding over time

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum amount for SIP?

Most mutual funds allow SIP starting from ₹500 per month.

Is SIP safe?

SIP invests in mutual funds, which are market-linked. It helps reduce risk through disciplined investing.

Can I stop SIP anytime?

Yes, SIPs are flexible and can be stopped or modified anytime.
